Blog-to-Podcast Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Blog-to-Podcast Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our extension. By using the Blog-to-Podcast extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

The Blog-to-Podcast extension collects the following information:

  • Article Content: When you use the extension to convert an article to audio, we extract the text content of the article you are viewing. This content is sent to Murf.ai's text-to-speech API for conversion to audio.
  • API Key: If you provide your own Murf.ai API key, it is stored securely in your browser's local storage.
  • Preferences: Your preferences, such as voice selection, playback speed, and chunk size, are stored in your browser's local storage.
  • Converted Audio: URLs to audio files generated by Murf.ai are stored in your browser's local storage to enable offline playback.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Convert article text to speech using Murf.ai's API
  • Store your preferences for future use
  • Enable offline playback of converted articles
  • Improve the extension's functionality

Third-Party Services

The Blog-to-Podcast extension uses Murf.ai's text-to-speech API to convert article text to audio. When you use our extension, article text is sent to Murf.ai for processing. Please refer to Murf.ai's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle your data.

Data Storage

All data collected by the Blog-to-Podcast extension is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage APIs. We do not store any of your data on our servers. Your data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties except as described in this policy.

Data Retention

Your data is stored in your browser's local storage until you choose to clear it. You can clear cached articles and preferences at any time through the extension's options page.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have stored about you (all data is stored locally in your browser)
  • Delete your data by clearing the extension's cache through the options page
  • Opt out of using the extension at any time by disabling or uninstalling it

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
